The Fragmented World of Pet Care

Today, pet data is scattered across paper records, different vet clinics, and outdated microchip databases. This leads to critical delays and risks.

Paper Chaos

Recovery Gap

Standard microchips are often outdated. 1 in 3 pets will go missing in their lifetime.

Siloed Systems

Vets can't see records from other clinics, leading to redundant tests and delayed care.
