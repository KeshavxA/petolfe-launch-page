The Fragmented World

Pet care is dangerously fragmented, relying on paper records and siloed databases that fail when pets are most vulnerable.

  • • 1 in 3 pets go missing
  • • Records take days to transfer
  • • Critical data is often lost

The Digital Identity Metaphor

We shifted the focus from "Record Vault" to "Universal Identity."

Identity vs. Storage

Identity is permanent; records are transient. By building a "Digital Passport," we make the product essential for every life stage.

Universal Access

The ID acts as a "Single Source of Truth" for vets, parents, and first responders globally.
